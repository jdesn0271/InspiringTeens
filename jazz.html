Jazz Jennings is an American transgeneder, YouTube personality, spokesmodel, television personality and a lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender activist. At only 15years old Jazz is notable for being one of the youngest documented people to be identified as having gender identity disorder, and for being the youngest person to become a national transgender figure. Jazz was first discovered in 2007 when an interview with Barbara Walters which aired on a show called 20/20. Since then jazz has been an inspiration for trangender teens all around, jazz sends a message to not only gays and transgenders but also their families by spreading a message of tolerance, acceptance and unconditional love. She inpires young adults to express themselves to others and be who you are and who you choose to be no matter what others may think and don’t be afraid to tell your family because they have unconditional love and will love you reguardless. Jazz contiues to take a stand for gays lebians and trangenders on social media and also on her reality tv show “ I Am Jazz”.
